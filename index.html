About Me

As the media environment changes opportunities for traditional journalists to experiment have presented themsleves.

I am a traditionally trained journalist who has over ten years expernience in local, regional and national newspapers.

Journalists by their nature are curious. The digital revolution has presented opportunites to be creative across a range of new media. I have put projects together with Paddy Power, Saatchi & Saatchi and I currently work at the intersection of media and technology for Google. This site is an effort to gather some of these projects together.
